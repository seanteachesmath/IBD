  • ‘Discrete logistic’ or Ricker model
    \begin{equation*} N_{t+1}=N_te^{r(1-\frac{N_t}{K})}, \end{equation*}
    Compare to our parameterization
    \begin{equation*} N_{t+1}=rN_te^{-kN_t}. \end{equation*}
  • Hassell model
    \begin{equation*} N_{t+1}=\frac{aN_t}{(1+bN_t)^c}. \end{equation*}
  • Beverton-Holt model
    \begin{equation*} N_{t+1}=\frac{\lambda N_t}{(1+\left(\frac{\lambda-1}{K}\right)N_t) }. \end{equation*}
