Guppies are fascinating, but cruel - they are quite happy to eat each other. It’s hard to track, but we know that as the guppy tank fills we see fewer baby guppies (i.e., per capita production drops as in Figure 6.2.1). For a model of guppy dynamics, we will assume
\begin{equation*} G_{t+1}=rG_t e^{-kG_t}. \end{equation*}
This model has been sketched above in the green curves of Figure 6.2.1 and Figure 6.3.2.
