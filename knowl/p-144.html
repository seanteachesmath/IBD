Since we are focusing on the fates of individual cells, it is perhaps important to think in terms of integer-valued states. Let \(N_0=n\) be the initial number of cells present, perhaps in come cell culture of a fascinating biological experiment. We should note that, while the process is discrete (in the number of cells), time is continuous.
