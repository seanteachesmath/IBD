We can establish some important information about solutions linked to properties of the matrix of coefficients
\begin{equation*} A = \begin{pmatrix}a\amp b\\c\amp d\end{pmatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Two important properties of this matrix are the trace
\begin{equation*} \tau = \operatorname{Tr}(A) = a + d, \end{equation*}
and the determinant
\begin{equation*} \Delta = \operatorname{Det}(A) = ad-bc. \end{equation*}
